# Project Overview

## Project Name
E-Commerce System Analysis & Design

## Business Problem
The company currently manages product information, customer orders, and order tracking through manual processes. This causes operational inefficiencies, inconsistent data management, and limited visibility into sales performance.

## Project Goal
Design an e-commerce system that supports customer ordering, order tracking, product management, and administrative operations through a centralized platform.

## Objectives
- Improve order processing efficiency
- Reduce manual workload
- Improve order visibility
- Standardize product and customer management
- Support future business growth

## Stakeholders
- Customer
- Sales Staff
- Admin
- Warehouse Staff
- Business Owner
- Development Team

## Success Criteria
- Customers can place orders online successfully.
- Admin can manage products and orders efficiently.
- Order status can be tracked throughout the lifecycle.
- Business users can access accurate order information.
